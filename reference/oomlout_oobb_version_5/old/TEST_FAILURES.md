## How failure reasons are captured

When a test fails, the per-test report includes a `Changed bits` section with concrete diff-style reasons (for example hash mismatches in `scad_hashes`, `json_hashes`, `yaml_hashes`, `txt_hashes`, or `openscad_ast_hashes`).

Failure reports are written to:

- `tests/test_runs/<test_name>/test_report.md`

And indexed in:

- [`tests/test_runs/README.md`](tests/test_runs/README.md)
